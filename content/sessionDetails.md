## Session Details

### **Sessions are offered online (including weekend availability) at £50 per session.**

I offer a complimentary 25 minute online session to see if working with me is the right fit for you.
This session will be less therapeutic and serve more as a getting-to-know-you session where you can ask any questions you may have about myself or the approach we would take to counselling. It is an opportunity to explore your expectations of counselling and what you hope to achieve. 

I currently work exclusively online via Teams and Zoom.

My counselling contracts are open-ended, meaning there is no pre-determined number of sessions, however we will always be working towards an eventual therapeutic end.

Reviews will be held every six sessions to assess how the therapy is going and if we feel it should continue.

Sessions are given weekly on a dedicated day/time which we will agree upon together.
