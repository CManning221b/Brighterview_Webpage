## About Declan

I’m a qualified integrative counsellor with a passion for helping people in times of difficulty and distress.

I focus on supporting people who are navigating grief and significant loss including the loss of family members, loved ones, jobs and relationships.

I have worked with individuals experiencing loss, anxiety, depression, challenging relationships and social issues. I strive to create an environment where you feel truly heard and understood whilst establishing a safe and caring professional relationship built upon empathy, trust and mutual respect.

I operate in a predominantly Person-Centred way, whilst drawing from CBT and collaborative Pluralistic practices. This allows us to work in tandem, towards your goals, whilst promoting positive change, introspection and personal growth.

I am someone who has struggled with feelings of low self-esteem, and I have found that effective therapy can often be the first step towards appreciating you for you.

I fundamentally believe that we are all valuable, unique and worthy of celebration. This includes accepting and celebrating ourselves.
