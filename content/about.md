## About Our Services

Our approach is grounded in creating a trusting and supportive therapeutic relationship, where feeling heard, understood, and accepted can help foster meaningful change.

When you are experiencing grief, bereavement, or other forms of loss, it can sometimes feel overwhelming or isolating. Having the opportunity to speak with someone who listens with care and understanding can help you make sense of your experiences. As person-centred informed integrative therapists, we can offer you this opportunity.
